FAQs

What is a form?

Use forms to sell services, receive reservations and capture information.

What are the transaction fees?

Blackbell transaction fees depend on your plan and are charged only for orders with online payments. You will be charged an additional payment fee of 2.9% + 30 cents used to process your customers credit cards.

Can I create a multi vendor marketplace?

Yes. You need to select the Business plan. You can create up to 125 internal web pages. That being said, you can invite an unlimited number of suppliers - their pages do not count in your pages limit.

Will my credit card be charged when my trial period is finished?

No. We don't ask for your credit card upfront, so you'll only be charged if you decide to upgrade to a paid plan.
